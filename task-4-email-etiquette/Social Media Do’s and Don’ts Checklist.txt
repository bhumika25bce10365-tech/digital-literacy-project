# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- 

## Do’s
- Think before posting anything online.
- Use privacy settings to keep your account safe.
- Share information only if you are sure it is correct.
- Be respectful while interacting with others.
- Try to keep your profile clean and somewhat professional.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Don’ts
- Don’t share personal details like phone number, OTP, etc.
- Don’t post anything offensive or inappropriate
- Avoid getting into unnecessary online fights or arguments
- Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links
- Don’t overshare every detail of your personal life
