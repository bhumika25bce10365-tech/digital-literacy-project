# Stay Safe Online – Checklist
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

- Don’t share your OTP, passwords, or personal details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank
  
- Always check links before clicking, especially if they look suspicious or too good to be true
   
- Keep your social media accounts private and avoid accepting random friend requests
  
- Think before posting anything online, because it can stay there permanently
   
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for important work like banking or logging into accounts  

- For UPI payments, double-check the receiver’s name before sending money
  
- Never scan unknown QR codes, as they can lead to fraud or money loss  

- Use strong passwords and try not to keep the same password for everything
  
- If something feels wrong, trust your instincts and don’t proceed further  

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- In case of any cybercrime, report it on https://cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930 immediately
